This website will be about different kinds of eyes! Yaah

Alcoholic eyes are often bloodshot, twitching or dry. Some things you might experience are cataracts, double vision and loss of contrast.

Dilated eyes, or mydriasis are often caused by adrenaline, head injuries, or neurological conditions. Sometimes, in the dark, our eyes dilate to let in more light.

Bloody eyes are from severe high blood pressure, bleeding disorders, forceful sneezing/coughing or eye infections.
